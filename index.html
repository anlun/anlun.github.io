I am the head of Programming Languages and Tools Lab at JetBrains Research and an adjunct professor at Constructor University Bremen. I was an associate professor at Higher School of Economics (November 2018 - June 2022) and a postdoctoral researcher at MPI-SWS hosted by Viktor Vafeiadis (June 2019 - October 2020), I did my PhD at St. Petersburg University, Russia. a research intern at IMDEA Software Institute, Madrid (June - August 2015) and at MPI-SWS, Kaiserslautern (May - August 2016; January, May, September - October 2017, September - October 2018). I'm interested in weak memory models and language semantics.
