I am a researcher at JetBrains Research and a docent at Higher School of Economics. I did my PhD at Saint Petersburg University, Russia. I was a research intern at IMDEA Software Institute, Madrid (June - August 2015) and at MPI-SWS, Kaiserslautern (May - August 2016; January, May, September - October 2017, September - October 2018). I'm interested in weak memory models and language semantics.
